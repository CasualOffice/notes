Everything, in one place

Four tools that finally work as one.

A note becomes a task. A task carries a reminder. A meeting becomes a note. Nothing is stranded in a separate app.

Notes that connect

A calm block editor with a daily-note journal, wiki-links, tags, and backlinks. Your thinking links itself together instead of scattering into folders.

Plans that stay clear

Today, Upcoming, Anytime, and Someday — with projects and areas. Capture a task in natural language and it lands where it belongs.

Reminders you can trust

Say “remind me every Monday at 9” and it recurs. A resilient scheduler delivers alerts whether the app is open or closed, and catches up on anything missed.

Meetings, understood

Record and transcribe on your device, then get summaries, decisions, and action items from a local model — each fact linked to the moment it was said.

One search for all of it

Full-text and semantic search across notes, tasks, and transcripts, with a command palette to go anywhere and do anything in a keystroke.

An assistant that cites

Ask questions across your whole knowledge base and get answers grounded in your own notes — with citations, or an honest “I don’t know.”

One foundation

Four pillars. One private core.

Every pillar sits on a single encrypted store, a shared link graph, and one search index — so your knowledge is genuinely connected, and entirely yours.

Private by architecture

Not a privacy setting. A privacy guarantee.

Recording, transcription, AI, search, and storage all run with the internet switched off. The network is touched only to download models and check for updates — with your consent.

Offline by defaultEvery core workflow is verified to run with no network connection.
Encrypted at restAn encrypted store whose key lives in your operating system’s keychain.
Zero telemetryThe shipped app is scanned in CI to prove it reports to no one.
On-device AISpeech and language models run locally. Your audio never leaves the machine.
Open sourceApache-2.0 licensed. Audit it, fork it, and keep it for good.
Your file, your formatOne portable, encrypted file. Export to Markdown, PDF, or JSON anytime.
