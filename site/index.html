One app, one brain

Four pillars, unified — not four apps stitched together

Everything shares one encrypted store, one search index, one link graph, and one evidence-citing AI workspace. A task links to the note that spawned it; a meeting becomes a note; a reminder targets any of them.

✍️ A notebook that thinks in blocks

A fast block/Markdown editor with wiki-links, backlinks, tags, and a daily-note journal — your everyday surface and the spine everything attaches to.

🗣️ Type it the way you'd say it

“remind me every Monday 9am”, “#idea call Sam next Friday” — natural-language capture routes to a note, task, or reminder, and never invents a date it isn't sure about.

🎧 Meetings, understood locally

Capture app + mic audio, transcribe on-device with Whisper, and generate summaries, decisions, and action items with a local LLM — every fact linked to transcript evidence.

🔎 Ask your whole knowledge base

Hybrid full-text + semantic search and an AI workspace that answers with citations — or honestly says it doesn't know, rather than making something up.

🔁 Reminders you can trust

A belt-and-suspenders scheduler fires alerts whether the app is open or closed, catches up on anything missed, and is honest about platform limits.

💾 Your data, one encrypted file

SQLite + SQLCipher on your disk. Crash-safe by design. Export to Markdown, PDF, or JSON anytime. It's your notebook — you own it.

Privacy by design, not by setting

Nothing leaves your device

The network is touched by exactly two named, consent-gated services: downloading AI models and checking for app updates. Everything else — recording, transcription, AI, search, storage — works with the internet switched off.

No cloud, no accountFully offline after model install. No sign-up, ever.
Encrypted at restSQLCipher store; the key lives in your OS keychain.
Zero telemetry by defaultThe shipped binary is scanned to prove it phones home to no one.
On-device AIWhisper + a local LLM. Your audio and notes never hit an API.
Open sourceApache-2.0. Audit it, fork it, run it forever.
Honest about limitsPlatform constraints are reported, never silently worked around.
